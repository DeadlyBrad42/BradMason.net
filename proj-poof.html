About

Poof is a command line tool for saving and retrieving Imgur links. It was written in C# and designed to use an Access database.

Before you can start retrieving Imgur links, Poof needs to associate tags with your pictures. You can do this easily by typing:

poof -t thumbsup.gif thumbs up

To retrieve the link, all you need to do is type:

poof thumbs up

And Poof will automatically copy the link and put it on your clipboard, ready to be pasted. It's easy!

Poof is a pretty neat little program, and I have a ton of ideas to improve it. I expect to be updating it in the near future.
